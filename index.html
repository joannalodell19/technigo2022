What is SEO?

A short summary

SEO (Search Engine Optimization) aims to get targeted traffic from a search engine's organic rankings. In other words you can say that using SEO is a way to get your website to show up in the results on search engines such as Google. You can do this in multiple ways. Common tasks include creating high-quality content and optimizing that content around specific keywords, and building backlinks. Driving more organic traffic to your website.
